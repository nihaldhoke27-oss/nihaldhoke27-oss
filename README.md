## 📖 Top Research Insights

> **01.** Founder content generates **8–10× more engagement** than company page content — invest in people, not pages.

> **02.** **40–60% of LinkedIn-influenced pipeline** is invisible in CRM attribution — dark social systematically undercounts organic ROI.

> **03.** **Comments outweigh likes 10:1** in LinkedIn's algorithm — design posts to start conversations, not just collect approval.

> **04.** **Specificity is a growth multiplier** — the narrower your niche, the stronger your content magnet for the right audience.

> **05.** **Links in posts reduce reach by 50–80%** — always put links in the first comment, never in the post body.
